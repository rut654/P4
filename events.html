Events

We have various events at the restaurant. Events are based on the occasion.

We are now accepting inquiries for private events and holiday parties, on weeknights and on weekends.

Normally we can accommodate up to 50 guests. Need advance request to accomodate more guests. We can provide DJ on special request.

Happy Hour

This event is open every day from 4:00pm to 6:00pm. Bookings can be done from 1 week to a day in advance and is subject to availability.

New Year's eve celebrations

Participation in this event needs to be booked two months in advance. Once we reach 100 bookings, we put people on waiting list.

Birthday Parties

This event needs to be booked 1 month in advance and can accomodate upto 50 guests. Order for the cake needs to be placed two weeks in advance.

Graduation Parties

This event needs to be booked 1 month in advance and can accomodate upto 50 guests. Order for the cake needs to be placed two weeks in advance.

Engagement Parties

Engagement parties need six months advance bookings. We can make arrangements to go upto 100 guests.

Please visit our Contact page and provide us your details, so you can get updates from us on upcoming events.
