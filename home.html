Welcome to TAJ Aroma House

The restaurant is located in the downtown of Morristown, New Jersey and is easily accessible. TAJ Aroma House was founded in October, 2012 and is a family-owned business.

Our talented chefs bring the aroma directly from the kitchen in India to the Kitchen in US. We use authentic Indian spices in all our food preparation.

For customers interested in sightseeing in India, more information is available at the front desk. You can also visit our travel page.

Now Accepting Inquiries for private events & Holiday Parties
